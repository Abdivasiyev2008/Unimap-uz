About the Program
Most students see the English section as the most challenging part of the test. This program, however, will make SAT preparation seamless, engaging, and fun. Adapted to the new digital format, this program aims to polish your theoretical understanding of SAT Writing and Reading concepts through consistent test practices and extensive feedback from your teacher.

You will learn to examine difficult reading passages and tackle demanding SAT Writing questions. While doing peer review sessions and collaborating in small groups, you will read and analyze academic texts preparing you for the exam and the undergraduate experience ahead.
